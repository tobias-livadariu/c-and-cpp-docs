## Baseline Choices

- C++17 is the default target for these notes because it is broadly supported by online judges and includes structured bindings, `optional`, `string_view`, `filesystem`, and many useful library improvements.
- C++20 and newer features are called out when useful, but the core templates avoid relying on them.
- The term STL is used pragmatically. Some files cover the broader C++ standard library because that is how the term is commonly used in interview and CP contexts.
